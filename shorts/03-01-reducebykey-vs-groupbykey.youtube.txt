reduceByKey vs groupByKey — why it matters

Both shuffle — the question is how much. reduceByKey combines values locally on each partition first (map-side combine), then shuffles only the partial results. groupByKey ships every value across the network, flooding the shuffle and risking out-of-memory. Aggregating? Always reach for reduceByKey. Same idea carries to groupBy().agg() on DataFrames.
