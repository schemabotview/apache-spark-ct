Why doesn't your Spark code run?

Transformations — filter, select, groupBy, join — build the recipe; nothing runs. Actions — count, show, write — trigger the whole chain at once. Waiting lets Spark see the entire plan: push filters early, drop unused columns, fuse steps. Transformation builds. Action triggers.
