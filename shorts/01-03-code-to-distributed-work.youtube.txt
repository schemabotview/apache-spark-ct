What happens when you hit run() in Spark?

Your DataFrame code becomes a logical plan. Catalyst rewrites it — filter pushdown, join reorder. The physical plan becomes a DAG of stages. Each stage splits into tasks — one per partition. Tasks run on the executors, in parallel.
