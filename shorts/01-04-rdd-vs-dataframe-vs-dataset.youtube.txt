RDD vs DataFrame vs Dataset — which one?

RDD — the original low-level API: full control, no schema, no Catalyst. DataFrame — a distributed table; Catalyst optimizes it. The default. Dataset — typed, compile-time safe, but JVM-only (not in Python). Default to DataFrames. Drop to RDD only when you must.
