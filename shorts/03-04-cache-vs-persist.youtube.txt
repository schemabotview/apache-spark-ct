Your Spark job is computing everything twice

By default, every action re-runs the whole lineage from source — .count() then .collect() on the same RDD computes it twice. Fix it with .cache() (materialize on first action, reuse after) or .persist(level) to pick the policy: MEMORY_ONLY, MEMORY_AND_DISK, DISK_ONLY, serialized (*_SER), replicated (*_2). Call .unpersist() when done — a cache holds executor memory until released.
