What's the most expensive line in Spark?

Narrow ops (map, filter, select) use only their own partition — no data moves. Wide ops (groupBy, join, distinct) need other partitions, forcing a shuffle. Every shuffle is a new stage — data crossing the network. Removing a shuffle is the #1 tuning win in Spark.
